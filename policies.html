Shipping & Returns

Shipping

Orders are typically processed within 1–3 business days. Shipping times vary depending on the item and carrier, and estimated delivery windows will be shown at checkout when available.

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation with tracking information where applicable. Murnan Auto is not responsible for carrier delays once a package has shipped.

Returns & Exchanges

We want you to be happy with what you order. If something isn't right, contact us within 30 days of delivery at support@murnanauto.store and we'll work with you on a return, exchange, or refund.

  • Items must be unused and in original condition/packaging unless defective.
  • Custom, made-to-order, or print-on-demand merch (such as embroidered apparel) may not be eligible for return unless defective or damaged in transit.
  • Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the item arrived defective or incorrect.

Damaged or Incorrect Items

If your order arrives damaged or you received the wrong item, email us with your order number and a photo of the item — we'll get it corrected as quickly as possible.
